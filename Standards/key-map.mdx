### Summary:

Keymaps are necessary to give contractors/reviewers on the relative location of your plan views throughout the construction sheets.

### Strategy:

This workflow allows making it possible to make updates to your keymap at any part of the project and to retain existing KeyMap references in all of your drawings. We are using

        6. Select the rectangle

        Select the blocks in the cluster and "Ref Edit" or "Edit Block in Place" and trim the linework that is extending past the boundary area
      </Step>

      <Step title="Detailed property definitions">
        Fine tune the block position and scale to where all of your contents fit cleanly inside your area with a max padding area of 0.1 from the legend base and border

        1. Adjust **_Position X_** to a number at a precision of **_0.<u>00</u>_**
        2. Adjust **_Position Y_** to a number at a precision of **_0.<u>00</u>_**
        3. Adjust the **_Scale Factor_** to an even number at a precision of **_0.<u>0000</u>_**
      </Step>
      <Step title="Document block properties">
        Create an Mtext and copy the block properties into this with the following format below:

        X: 00.00\
        Y: 00.00\
        S: 0.0000

        You can then Cut this out and place inside of your working keymap drawing and scale to a larger text height to locate for future additions
      </Step>

  <Accordion title="Modifying your KeyMap Block">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open source and destination drawings">
        - Open your KeyMap\_Update - Project ID.dwg
        - Open the master file with the changes
      </Step>
      <Step title="Copy and paste from source to destination">
        1. Copy all of the linework from your master file\
           **_Keep in your temporary clipboard_**
        2. Proceed to open the corresponding block that is the same name as your source drawing name
        3. Select similar of all the linework that you are replacing
        4. Delete linework
        5. Paste original coordinates in the block
        6. Save and close block

        <Tip>
          To use select similar easier: make sure to do a **_PEDIT_** → **_Multiple_** → then select all of your source linework to not leave any stragglers
        </Tip>
      </Step>
      <Step title="Update KeyMap block in titleblock.dwg">
        1. Copy your KeyMap block and your Mtext with block property data\
           **_Keep in your temporary clipboard_**
        2. Delete KeyMap Block
        3. Navigate to the drawing performance panel and purge all
        4. Paste your block into drawing
      </Step>
      <Step title="Save and proceed">
        1. Click on your block while looking at the Mtext data
        2. Update your KeyMap Block based on the data
        3. Delete your Mtext data block
        4. Save and Proceed
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Accordion>
